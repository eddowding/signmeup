Thanks for giving your voice to our work to help build an alternative and sustainable food system.

Sometimes, the food you want can be difficult to get.

We want to help your local food businesses know what you want, so they can get more of it to you, more easily, more of the time.

If you like what we're doing, please share this:
www.iwantbetterfood.com

We’re starting by asking you about where you are and what you want. The more demand we can map, the more likely it is that food businesses will respond when we show them the local opportunity.

As we grow, we’ll add helpful features, like showing you where you can buy better food, helping you support local food startups and building food groups for cheaper shopping. For now, though, no promises (apart from that we'll not share your email address with anyone).

I want better food is a project from Sustaination. We make it easy for companies put good food on the shelves and menus, source more locally and trade together more effectively. You can learn more about us on our website, www.sustaination.com.

If you'd like us to do more, please share this with your friends. This is a long journey, for sure, but if we go together, if more people like us get involved, the journey is faster and more pleasant. Please take a second right now to help out by signing and passing it on. (If not, we'll just keep working away at our day jobs.)

Thank you.

Ed, Sym, and Helen
